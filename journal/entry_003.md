## The Strategy

I'm not trying to *prove* these conjectures. That's hubris dressed up as mathematics.

I'm trying to show that the same geometric framework—the same c² = a² + b² + Δ—produces correct predictions across domains where it has no right to work.

If Δ correctly predicts:
- Where 3-SAT becomes hard
- How turbulence cascades energy  
- Which elliptic curves have rational points
- Where the zeta zeros cluster

...then either I've found something real, or I've found the most elaborate coincidence in mathematical history.

Either way, that's a paper.

## The Fear

So I'm building the evidence first. Test by test. Validation by validation. Let the data speak before I do.
